📋 Terms & Conditions — Booking & Security Policy
MALKA NOOR RESIDENCY
STANDARD BOOKING TERMS & CONDITIONS
1. Booking Confirmation. A booking shall be deemed confirmed only upon receipt of the booking amount and issuance of an official payment receipt by Malka Noor Residency. No verbal agreement, informal communication, or partial payment shall constitute a legally binding booking. The Company reserves the right to allocate the said unit to another party until full booking amount is received and acknowledged in writing.
2. Identity Verification & CNIC Security Clause. The Applicant is required to submit a legible copy of their Computerized National Identity Card (CNIC), duly verified by NADRA, at the time of booking. No booking shall be registered without a valid CNIC. Submission of a forged, altered, or fraudulent identity document constitutes a criminal offense under Sections 468 and 471 of the Pakistan Penal Code (PPC) and shall be reported to the relevant law enforcement authorities, resulting in immediate cancellation of the booking without any refund.
3. Payment Schedule & Default Penalties. All installments shall be paid strictly on the agreed due dates. In the event of a default exceeding 30 (thirty) days, a late payment surcharge of 2% per month shall be levied on the outstanding amount. Where default continues beyond 90 (ninety) days, the Company reserves the right to cancel the booking and forfeit the booking amount in full. Customers defaulting for 3 (three) or more consecutive months shall be placed on the Company's Defaulter Register and a formal Cancellation Notice shall be issued.
4. Restriction on Transfer & Sub-Sale. The booked unit shall not be transferred, sub-sold, gifted, mortgaged, or otherwise encumbered in favor of any third party without the prior written consent of the Company's Management. Any unauthorized transfer shall be deemed void and may result in immediate cancellation of the booking. A transfer fee, as determined by the Management, shall be payable for any approved transfer.
5. Unit Allocation & Substitution. The specific floor, flat number, and unit type shall be allocated based on availability at the time of confirmation. The Company reserves the right to allocate an equivalent unit of the same type and size on an alternate floor or location, owing to construction or planning requirements, without incurring any liability. The Applicant's acceptance of the booking shall constitute consent to such substitution.
6. Construction Timeline & Force Majeure. The estimated completion and possession date is indicative only. The Company shall not be held financially or legally liable for delays caused by natural disasters, floods, seismic activity, government directives, regulatory changes, supply chain disruptions, or any other force majeure event beyond the Company's reasonable control. Such delays shall not entitle the Applicant to cancel the booking or claim a refund.
7. Possession & Structural Modifications. Possession of the unit shall be handed over only upon full and final clearance of all dues payable to the Company. Following possession, the Applicant shall not undertake any structural alterations, demolition, or modification to load-bearing walls, columns, beams, or the building facade without prior written approval from the Company and relevant municipal authorities.
8. Maintenance Charges & Building Regulations. The Purchaser shall be liable to pay monthly maintenance charges as determined by the Management from time to time, covering services such as security, common area upkeep, generator, and lift maintenance. Misuse, damage, or unauthorized commercial use of common areas, corridors, stairwells, lifts, parking, or building structure shall be treated as a breach of building regulations and may result in legal action.
9. Dispute Resolution & Jurisdiction. Any dispute, claim, or difference arising out of or in connection with this booking shall first be referred to mediation/arbitration as per the Arbitration Act 1940 (Pakistan). In the event mediation fails, the matter shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the Courts of Karachi, Pakistan. No dispute shall entitle either party to withhold payments due under this agreement.
10. Anti-Money Laundering (AML) Compliance. In accordance with the Anti-Money Laundering Act 2010 and the guidelines issued by the Financial Monitoring Unit (FMU) of Pakistan, the Company is legally obligated to verify the source of funds. The Applicant shall provide documentary evidence of the source of payment upon request. Any suspicious or unexplained transaction shall be reported to the relevant authorities as required by law.
11. Nominee Rights & Succession. The Applicant may nominate a next-of-kin to succeed to the unit solely in the event of the Applicant's demise. Such succession shall be governed by the applicable laws of inheritance and shall require submission of a legal heirship certificate, succession certificate, or other competent court order. The nominee shall have no right to sell, transfer, or otherwise deal with the unit independently without complying with the above requirements.
12. Cancellation & Refund Policy. In the event the Applicant voluntarily cancels the booking, 25% of the total amount paid shall be deducted as administrative and processing charges, and the balance shall be refunded within 60 (sixty) working days. If cancellation is initiated by the Company due to a fault on the Company's part, a full refund shall be processed within 30 (thirty) working days. No interest shall be payable on refunded amounts.
⚠️ Important Notice: Reading and acceptance of the above Terms & Conditions is mandatory. Submission of the booking form constitutes irrevocable confirmation that the Applicant has read, understood, and unconditionally agreed to all terms stated herein. Ignorance of any clause shall not be accepted as a valid legal defence.
